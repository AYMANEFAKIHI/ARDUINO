Project #42: Stepper Motor Basic Steps (28BYJ-48)

Stepper motors are different from DC motors because they move in precise "steps". This makes them perfect for 3D printers, CNC machines, and clocks. The 28BYJ-48 is a common, inexpensive stepper motor that comes with a ULN2003 driver board.

1. Concept and Description

Stepper motors are different from DC motors because they move in precise "steps". This makes them perfect for 3D printers, CNC machines, and clocks. The 28BYJ-48 is a common, inexpensive stepper motor that comes with a ULN2003 driver board.

2. Components Needed

  • Arduino Uno Board
  • USB Cable
  • 28BYJ-48 Stepper Motor
  • ULN2003 Driver Board
  • Jumper Wires
  • External 5V Power (Recommended, but USB can often handle one small stepper)

3. Physical Montage (Wiring Instructions)

Connect the ULN2003 driver board to the Arduino:

  • IN1 to Arduino Pin 8
  • IN2 to Arduino Pin 9
  • IN3 to Arduino Pin 10
  • IN4 to Arduino Pin 11
  • VCC (+) to Arduino 5V
  • GND (-) to Arduino GND
  • Plug the motor connector into the white socket on the driver board.
