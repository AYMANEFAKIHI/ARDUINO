Project #37: Servo Motor Basic Sweep

Servo motors are precise actuators that can rotate to a specific angle (usually between 0 and 180 degrees). They are essential for robotics, allowing you to move arms, steer wheels, or position sensors. In this project, we will make a servo sweep back and forth automatically.

1. Concept and Description

Servo motors are precise actuators that can rotate to a specific angle (usually between 0 and 180 degrees). They are essential for robotics, allowing you to move arms, steer wheels, or position sensors. In this project, we will make a servo sweep back and forth automatically.

2. Components Needed

  • Arduino Uno Board
  • USB Cable
  • Micro Servo Motor (SG90 or similar)
  • Jumper Wires

3. Physical Montage (Wiring Instructions)

Connect the servo motor wires as follows:

  • Red Wire (Power) to Arduino 5V
  • Brown/Black Wire (Ground) to Arduino GND
  • Orange/Yellow Wire (Signal) to Arduino Pin 9
