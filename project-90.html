Project #90: Bluetooth Robot Car Control

Control a 2-wheel drive robot car using your smartphone.

1. Concept and Description

Combine motor control (L298N) with Bluetooth (HC-05) to drive a robot.

2. Components Needed

  • Arduino Uno
  • L298N Driver
  • 2 DC Motors
  • HC-05
  • Chassis

3. Physical Montage (Wiring Instructions)

Motors to L298N. L298N IN1-IN4 to Arduino D4-D7. HC-05 to D2, D3.
